💦 Services
Complete Exterior
Cleaning & Sealing.

From driveways to home exteriors — Infinity handles it all with the fast, efficient professionalism Sarasota customers trust.

🏠
House Washing
Fresh exterior from top to bottom.
Professional pressure washing of home exteriors — removing mold, mildew, algae, and built-up grime from siding, stucco, and painted surfaces.
🚗
Driveway Cleaning
Like new in one visit.
Deep pressure washing of concrete and paver driveways. Removes oil stains, tire marks, mold, and years of built-up Florida grime in a single efficient visit.
🔒
Concrete & Paver Sealing
Protect your investment.
Professional sealing of driveways, walkways, and patios after cleaning. Sealing prevents future staining, extends surface life, and enhances appearance.
🏊
Pool Deck Cleaning
Safe, slip-free, and spotless.
Pool deck pressure washing removes algae, calcium deposits, and slippery buildup. Safe for all deck materials including pavers, concrete, and tile.
🧱
Sidewalk & Walkway Cleaning
Curb appeal starts at the path.
Pressure washing of sidewalks, walkways, and entryways. Makes a powerful first impression and keeps your property looking sharp year-round.
