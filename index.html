✂️ Services
Full Grooming Care
for Your Fur Baby.

From a simple bath and brush to a full groom — Kelly handles every breed with the same gentle, thorough attention to detail.

✂️
Full Grooming
Bath, cut, dry & style.
Complete grooming including bath, blow dry, breed-appropriate haircut, ear cleaning, nail trim, and finishing touches. Kelly takes her time and does it right.
🛁
Bath & Brush
Fresh, clean & fluffy.
Professional bath, blow dry, and thorough brush-out. Perfect for maintenance between full grooms. Leaves your dog smelling amazing.
💅
Nail Trim
Quick, safe & painless.
Careful nail trimming and filing. Kelly's calm approach makes even nail-shy dogs comfortable throughout the process.
👂
Ear Cleaning
Healthy ears, happy dog.
Gentle ear cleaning to remove buildup and prevent infections. Essential for long-eared and floppy-eared breeds especially in Florida's humid climate.
🐾
Paw Treatment
Soft paws, every time.
Paw pad trimming and conditioning. Keeps paws healthy and comfortable, especially on Florida's hot pavement.
🌸
Breed-Specific Styling
Looking their best.
Expert styling for all breeds — ShiZu, Maltese, Malshi, English Setter, and more. Kelly knows how to make every dog look and feel their best.
